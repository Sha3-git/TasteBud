# STAKEHOLDER MANAGEMENT PLAN

**Project Name:** TasteBud


| **Name** | **About the Stakeholder** | **Plan** |
|-----------|----------------------------|-----------|
| Users (People with or want to track food allergies) | Primary users of the app who rely on it to identify allergens, track reactions, and receive alerts on potential cross-reactivity. They value reliability, simplicity, and appreciate a high degree of accuracy. | Conduct surveys and beta testing to gather feedback on usability. Provide clear onboarding tutorials and continuous updates based on user reports. Maintain open communication channels for support and improvement. |
| Doctors / Allergists | Medical professionals who can view user reports to help interpret patterns and provide treatment recommendations. Their focus is on accuracy and data gathering. | Develop a secure and simple to follow reporting interface for users who have reported their meals for a specific amount of days (streak) to ensure maximum accuracy for professionals. |
