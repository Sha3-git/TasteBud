# Business Model Analysis

Allergen and food intolerance symptom tracking application that eases the difficulty in narrowing down allergens by utilizing the meal logs and symptoms to recognize patterns over time as well as cross reactivity.

### What is offered

* General daily food and symptom tracker  
* Symptom analysis and trend viewer  
* Unsafe, allergenic food viewer â†’ with associated reported symptoms  
*  Cross reactivity alert for possible allergen likelihoods

### Value Proposition 
* Simplifies the task of finding unknown allergies and intolerances
* Identifies correlations and risky foods that the user might not be aware of
* Provides insight into reaction occurrences not just their declaration
* Longterm tracking and history monitoring for the user

### To whom it is offered

* Individuals with mild to moderate food allergies or intolerances
* Parents or Guardians of children with suspected Sensitivities 
* Designed for personal awareness and not emergency or medical guidance in any way

### How it is offered
* offered on mobile and web devices.
* Cross-platform is to ensure availability and consistency of use 

### Why we capture value

* We shorten the journey to identifying triggering foods
* We also enable future partnerships or assistance to medical personel, food product databases, and food retail.
* Competitive differentiation of triggering foods through cross reactive pattern detection

| Alternative           | Shortcoming                | Where TasteBud wins         |
| --------------------- | -------------------------- | --------------------------- |
| Traditional diaries   | No insight, just recording | Automated pattern detection |
| Allergen test kits    | Expensive & limited        | More dynamic + continuous   |
| Fitness food trackers | Not medically relevant     | Symptom-based correlations  |

### Revenue Model
* Freemium app with premium analytics subsription
